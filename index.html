Nilas Housekeeping and Cleaning Services

NILAS Housekeeping & Cleaning Services Ltd. is made up of a group of industry professionals with five years of experience in the cleaning industry. We are not a franchise and we do not sell cleaning contracts.

Founded in 2015, we presently service hundreds of clients and millions of square feet each and every night of the week. This ranges from small once-a-week offices with just a few staff members to large clients with hundreds of staff.

Our fully-trained and uniformed cleaning crews maintain a top-notch professional image that will complement your business. All cleaning personnel are bonded and insured. In addition, all staff is security checked with the Saudi local police authorities ensuring that your building and inventory are always secure.

Nilas Resorts

Make sure the door to your room is always locked, and make it a habit to use all locks, including the deadbolt and chain. If you choose to stay in a room that faces the pools or beach, make sure your sliding doors and windows have sturdy locks.

Instruct children that only grown-ups should answer the door. Even when someone identifies himself as a RESORTS employee, only an adult should answer the door, and, if the visitor is not expected, call the from desk to confirm his or her status before letting them in.

Review the resort’s safety information with your children upon check-in; it’s in the bound RESORTS guide in your room or broadcast on the hotel’s TV. Make sure everyone knows how to find the nearest exit, where the staircase leads, and how to reach the front desk in case of emergency.
